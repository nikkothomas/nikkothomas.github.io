About Nikko

Nikko Thomas is an actor and artist from Phoenix Arizona. He graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelors in Theater (Concentration in Acting) from Arizona State University. Among several roles, Nikko appeared in Writes, a National Playwrights Conference Semifinalist at the Eugene O'Neill Theater Center. In 2014, he received the Best Actor Award at the Campus Movie Festival for the short film Brunch Call.
